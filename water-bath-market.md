<p><ul><li>Circulating Water Bath</li><li>Non-Circulating Water Bath</li><li>Shaking Water Bath</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water bath market includes three primary types: circulating, non-circulating, and shaking water baths. Circulating water baths ensure uniform temperature distribution by continuously moving water, making them ideal for precise temperature control. Non-circulating water baths provide stable temperatures without movement, suitable for less sensitive applications. Shaking water baths combine temperature control with agitation, enhancing sample mixing and reaction rates. Each type serves distinct laboratory needs, catering to various research and industrial applications.</p></p>
