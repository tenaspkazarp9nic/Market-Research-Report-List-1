<p><p>The Computer Numerical Control (CNC) software market is primarily divided into two types: CAD (Computer-Aided Design) and CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacturing) software. CAD software focuses on designing and modeling parts and assemblies in a digital environment, enabling precise visualization and adjustments. Conversely, CAM software translates these designs into machine instructions for automated fabrication processes, optimizing production efficiency.
