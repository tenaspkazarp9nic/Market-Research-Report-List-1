<p><p>The pet decavitamin market is categorized into two main types: fat-soluble and water-soluble vitamins. Fat-soluble vitamins, such as A, D, E, and K, are stored in the liver and fatty tissues, playing crucial roles in vision, bone health, and immune function. Water-soluble vitamins, including C and B-complex vitamins, are not stored in the body and must be regularly consumed to support energy metabolism, nervous system function, and overall health in pets. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal well-being in animals.</p></p>
